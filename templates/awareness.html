This demonstration shows how a login page may appear convincing. Before entering credentials, always verify:

  • ✔ Check the website address carefully.
  • ✔ Look for HTTPS and the correct domain.
  • ✔ Be cautious with urgent messages.
  • ✔ Never enter credentials from links in unexpected emails.
Check the URL

Always verify the website address before entering your username or password. Attackers often use domains that closely resemble legitimate ones.

Be Careful with Emails

Avoid clicking links in unexpected emails. Visit the official website directly instead of using embedded links.

Protect Credentials

Never share passwords or one-time passwords (OTPs). Legitimate organizations will not ask for them through email.
