Learning Objectives

Identify Red Flags

Look for urgent language, fake domains, suspicious links, spelling mistakes, and requests for sensitive information.

Think Before You Click

Always verify links before opening them and never trust unexpected requests for passwords or financial information.

Stay Protected

Enable multi-factor authentication, keep software updated, and report suspicious emails to your organization's security team.
